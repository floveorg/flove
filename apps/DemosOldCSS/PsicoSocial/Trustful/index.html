Scope of trust

Pick where you currently extend trust to your trustee. Add notes per item if needed. It's easier to declare one scope at a time, for getting deeper in it.

Why partial Trusting?

It’s normal to trust someone highly in one area and more cautiously in another. People are reliable in some domains and conditions.

TRUSTY frames trust as scoped, time-bound and evidence-aware — realistic and fair. Precise scopes make trust more robust. Specific domains avoid blanket claims.

Relationship & Time window

Why to tell about relation and choose a trust timeframe?

There are people that we don't know much and we will trust them forever and 100%, and the way round. You could complementarily specify your actual confidence in a field below.

Most trusts are only needed for a period time, and some people can have a higher degree of trust within a finite time. Trust can also be an experimental experience that could trigger amazing future experiences for one, for the other andor for both. Normally, with more unknown people we try short windows of time first, then we extend as we go accumulating assurance.

Protection

State some boundaries and that keep the trust healthy.

Why getting so specific?

Good boundaries are a gift for both and all 🎁. They reduce ambiguity and prevent resentment. Write them as if both sides must win, don't feel paranoid about being more open, bullies are not trustful people :)
