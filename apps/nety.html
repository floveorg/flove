What nety is

identity

Own your graph

Your identity is an Ed25519 keypair in an encrypted keystore — not a row in someone's database. Posts, profiles and follows are signed and yours to carry.

interop

Connect any network

A peer-to-peer social protocol over libp2p (gossipsub + Kademlia) moves posts and follows between peers. A GraphQL gateway lets apps speak to the mesh.

cooperative

Shared resources

Nodes offer compute, storage and AI to each other. A Web of Trust plus CRDT reputation decides who to rely on — no central operator required.

Today vs. the design

Being straight about it: the GraphQL gateway currently keeps feed, profiles and follows in an in-memory store (lost on restart) and doesn't yet talk to the P2P daemon. WebSocket subscriptions are in the schema but not served over the wire. The private-subnet PSK flag is accepted but not enforced at the transport layer yet. The mesh runs; wiring the social gateway into it is the work in flight.
