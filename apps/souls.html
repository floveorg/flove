What is Souls?

An old projective game in six breaths. An animal reveals how you move through the world, a colour the company you seek, a species what you long to inhabit, a sense what you taste of the world — and so on, deeper.

The portrait writes itself.

    ✺

    Welcome to Souls.

    A small mirror, taken in breaths.

    Old projective games invite the soul to speak through animals, colours, senses, and gestures. Souls is a quiet walk through that mirror — at your pace, in your own words.

    ✦

    Six stages. Each one a door.

    You start with an animal and a colour. From there you go deeper — a species you'd inhabit, a sense you'd live through, and on. Stop whenever it feels enough. Nothing is lost.

    ❉

    The first thing that arrives.

    Don't think too hard. Each question wants the first answer that comes — the one before the editor wakes up. The portrait writes itself.
