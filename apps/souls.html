What is Souls?

An old projective game in four breaths: an animal reveals how you move through the world, a colour the company you seek. Three words for each — fast, honest, without thinking too hard.

The portrait writes itself.

Step 1 of 4 · Animal

Think of an animal.

The first that comes to mind. Don't second-guess it.

Step 2 of 4 · What you offer

Three words about that animal.

These describe what you bring to others.

Step 3 of 4 · Colour

Now a colour.

Any colour you want — write it freely.

Step 4 of 4 · What you seek

Three qualities of that colour.

These describe what you'd like to find in others.

Your soul portrait

This is what you carry.

A small mirror — keep it, share it, or play again.
